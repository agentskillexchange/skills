# Pandoc Universal Document Converter

Pandoc is a free and open-source universal document converter that can translate between dozens of markup and document formats including Markdown, HTML, LaTeX, DOCX, PDF, EPUB, and more. It is the Swiss-army knife of document conversion for writers, researchers, and developers.
