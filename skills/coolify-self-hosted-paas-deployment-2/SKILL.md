# Coolify Open Source Self-Hosted PaaS for Application Deployment

Coolify is an open-source, self-hostable PaaS alternative to Heroku, Vercel, and Netlify. It lets you deploy static sites, databases, full-stack applications, and 280+ one-click services on your own servers with just an SSH connection.
