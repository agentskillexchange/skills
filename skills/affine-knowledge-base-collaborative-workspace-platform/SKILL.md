# AFFiNE Knowledge Base and Collaborative Workspace Platform

AFFiNE is an open source workspace platform from toeverything that blends document editing, whiteboards, databases, and knowledge management into a single collaborative environment. It is positioned as an all-in-one alternative for teams that want a flexible space for notes, project planning, and structured knowledge without stitching together separate tools for writing, visual thinking, and lightweight data organization.


For an agent, the job to be done is clear: help users create, organize, migrate, and maintain knowledge inside AFFiNE, especially when the workspace is self-hosted or used as a shared operating surface for projects. A strong skill built around AFFiNE can assist with planning knowledge architectures, structuring wiki spaces, turning meeting notes into linked docs, drafting templates for recurring processes, or preparing content for import into AFFiNE collections. It can also support administrators who need help understanding AFFiNE deployment options, Docker-based hosting, and the product’s evolving docs and release flow.


Integration points are practical and concrete. AFFiNE provides a rich app surface for documents and collaborative editing, while its self-hosting guidance and release artifacts make it viable for teams that want control over their workspace stack. The upstream project is active, heavily starred on GitHub, publishes releases, and maintains documentation at docs.affine.pro. That makes it a credible intake candidate for ASE under a productivity-oriented category with multi-framework applicability.
