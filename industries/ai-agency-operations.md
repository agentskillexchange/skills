# 🧭 AI Agency Operations & FDE Workflows

Client-facing AI delivery, agency operations, forward deployed engineering workflows, automation consulting, and reusable implementation systems for client work.

- Live page: https://agentskillexchange.com/industry-skills/#ai-agency-operations
- Homepage access: Curated Collections on https://agentskillexchange.com/

## Who this is for

- AI agency founders, forward deployed engineers, automation consultants, client delivery leads, and solutions engineers packaging repeatable AI-enabled services for clients.
- RevOps and marketing ops agencies that need site audits, CRM enrichment, campaign operations, deliverable production, and workflow QA.

## Jobs covered

- Audit client sites, docs, workflows, and campaign surfaces.
- Build repeatable client automation workflows across forms, webhooks, tools, and campaign systems.
- Package findings into reports, docs, spreadsheets, and handoff artifacts.
- Manage CRM, lead enrichment, newsletter, and marketing automation operations.
- Monitor delivery quality and reusable agency workflows.

## Workflow Stacks

- **Client audit sprint:** Crawl site → run SEO audit → summarize findings → build report → deliver action plan
- **Automation implementation:** Map workflow → connect tools → enrich records → trigger handoffs → validate outputs
- **Campaign operations:** Plan SEO pages → segment contacts → send campaigns → repair workbooks → document results
