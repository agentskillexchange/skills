# 📣 GTM & RevOps Workflows

Demand generation, SEO and content operations, lifecycle email, CRM enrichment, lead routing, scheduling, feedback capture, and sales/revenue operations workflows.

- Live page: https://agentskillexchange.com/industry-skills/#gtm-revops-workflows
- Homepage access: Curated Collections on https://agentskillexchange.com/

## Who this is for

- Demand generation, growth, and marketing operations teams building repeatable acquisition workflows.
- RevOps and CRM teams responsible for enrichment, routing, cleanup, scheduling, and sales handoff.
- Lifecycle marketers and content operators who need auditable campaign and SEO workflows, not one-off prompts.

## Jobs covered

- Plan SEO, content-gap, keyword, and technical audit workflows.
- Design lifecycle email, newsletter, and owned-audience campaign operations.
- Enrich CRM contacts, route leads, schedule handoffs, and keep revenue data usable.
- Capture customer feedback and route GTM signals into follow-up workflows.

## Workflow Stacks

- **Demand generation audit:** Research keywords → compare gaps → audit site quality → prioritize content plan
- **Lifecycle campaign build:** Segment audience → design sequence → send newsletter → measure feedback
- **Lead handoff flow:** Capture form → enrich contact → route in CRM → schedule follow-up → notify owner
- **RevOps cleanup sprint:** Export CRM records → bulk update data → dedupe contacts → document routing rules

## Editorial Notes

- This collection is practitioner-focused: use it for operational GTM workflows, not broad marketing strategy or unsupervised outbound.
- Vendor-family caps are intentional: Salesforce, HubSpot, Ahrefs, and SEMrush are limited to two primary picks each.
- SEMrush Content Optimizer is included as a listed-only option for teams already using SEMrush; review source, terms, and data handling before production use.
- Formbricks Survey and Experience Management was chosen over the narrower Formbricks survey-platform duplicate because the GTM workflow depends on lifecycle feedback and customer experience signals.
- Keep CRM writes, enrichment, email sending, and paid-search changes human-reviewed; avoid using these skills for spam, scraped outreach, or unauthorized contact processing.
